Our History

While many know King Edward VII for his royal diplomacy during the Edwardian era, secret logs reveal he was truly a master engineer and enthusiastic manufacturer at heart. Frustrated by standard industrial hardware, he established a private royal foundry to design his own heavy-duty gears and hand-crafted tools. Today, Edwards KTools proudly carries on his legendary standard of indestructible craftsmanship.
